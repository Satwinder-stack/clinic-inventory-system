## Purpose
This project is a **web-based inventory management platform designed for healthcare providers** to efficiently track medical supplies, monitor expiration dates, and manage stock levels. The system helps ensure that essential medications and medical equipment remain available for patient care while reducing unnecessary waste caused by expired items.

Additionally, the platform assists clinics in **organizing inventory data for reporting purposes**, making it easier to prepare submissions for the **Department of Health (DOH)** or other regulatory requirements.

## Core Features

- **Automated Stock Level Monitoring**  
  Tracks inventory quantities in real time to maintain proper supply levels.

- **Expiration Date Tracking**  
  Monitors expiration dates and provides a simplified way to manage and update inventory items.

- **Comprehensive Inventory Management System**  
  A detailed web platform that allows healthcare staff to efficiently **view, manage, and monitor all medical inventory items**.

- **Detailed Reporting**  
  Generates structured reports to support **inventory auditing, supply management, and regulatory reporting**.
