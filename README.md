## Purpose
This project is a **web-based inventory management platform designed for healthcare providers** to efficiently track medical supplies, monitor expiration dates, and manage stock levels. The system helps ensure that essential medications and medical equipment remain available for patient care while reducing unnecessary waste caused by expired items.

Additionally, the platform simplifies administrative processes by helping clinics **organize and track inventory data that may be required when submitting reports to the Department of Health (DOH)** or other regulatory bodies.

---

## Core Features

- **Automated Stock Level Monitoring**  
  Tracks inventory quantities in real time and helps maintain optimal supply levels.

- **Expiration Date Tracking**  
  Monitors expiration dates of medical supplies and provides an organized way to manage and update items.

- **Comprehensive Inventory Management**  
  A detailed web-based system that allows healthcare staff to efficiently **view, manage, and monitor all medical inventory items**.

- **Reporting and Performance Insights**  
  Generates structured reports to assist clinics with **inventory auditing, supply management, and regulatory reporting requirements**.
